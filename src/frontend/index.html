Kallbad Trip

About this project

Kallbad Trip started as a small student project built by three computer science students. We were fascinated by Sweden's tradition of cold-water bathing and wanted to explore it while learning how to design a database-backed web application.

This site aggregates official bathing spot data and makes it easy to discover places to take a dip in Sweden. Browse the map, click markers to view details, save favorites and leave local reviews to share your experience with others.
